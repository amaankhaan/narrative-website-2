OUR SOLAR SYSTEM

THE QUESTION WE FIRST WE FIRST NEED TO ANWSER IS, WHAT IS OUR SOLAR SYSTEM? FORMATION OF THE SOLAR SYTSTEM BEGAN ROUGHLY 4.6 BILLION YEARS AGO WITH HUGE AMOUNTS OF MASS BUILDING UP, CAUSING A GIANT GRAVIOTATIONAL COLLAPSE IN A SMALL AREA WITHIN A GIANT MOLECULAR CLOUD. A LARGE NUMBER OF THE MASS GATHERD WITHIN THE CENTRE OF THE CLOUD RESULTING IN NUCLEAR FUSION REACTIONS OF HYDROGEN CONVERTING INTO HELIUM PRODUCING A STAR – WHICH NOW IS KNOWN AS OUR SUN. AS A RESULT, LARGE SPIRILING CLOUDS FLATTEND INTO PROTOPLANETARY DISKS, OUT OF WHICH ROCKS, METEROIDS AND SPACE DEBRIS CLUMPED AND FORMED TOGETHER UNDER INTESE HEAT AND PRESSURE. MILLIONS OF YEARS LATER 8 SOLESTIAL BODIES ORBIT THE SUN – ALONG WITH RINGS OF ASTROIDS (KUIPER BELT)- WHICH AS A WHOLE ARE ALSO ORBITING THE CENTRE OF THE MILKYWAY AT A SPEED OF 450 THOUSAND MILES PER HOUR, BUT EVEN AT THIS SPEED IT TAKES OVER 250 MILLION YEARS TO COMPLETE A FULL ORBIT!
