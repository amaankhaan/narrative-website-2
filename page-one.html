Elon Musk

, founder of 6 major businesses; Tesla, Space X, Neural ink the Boring company, and now acquiring Twitter. Musk is the second richest man, with a net worth of $181 Billion dollars. Space x, was founded and formed by owner Elon musk in 2002, following his success with Tesla- automated car driving company, In hopes of revolutionising the space travel industry and manufacturing an affordable spaceflight experience for major companies. Gaining worldwide attention, along with collaborations with NASA, it’s the only space organisation capable of a low earth orbit, in 2010 space X set records when launching a full payload within the earth’s orbit and programmed the vessel to return the ground, safety

What is Space X currently doing?

In the present time, along with leading world class astrophysics, engineering research. Space X is committed to improving the life of the everyday user. The multinational company is designing and building safe, reliable and demisable satellites for their high reliability along with investigating the second gen’ Moon astronauts which will be the first to fly in the Artemis 4 mission (Space X x NASA) in 2027.

Space X future goal?

We all dream of what it would be like on different planets, by 2023 Space X promises to inhabit and send the first of the human civilisation to set foot on mars, via starship. In the future, Space X plans its missions to send small fleets (also known as Starship mini!) funded completely by public/private partnerships which brings the hope of built infrastructure and colonization the red planet.
