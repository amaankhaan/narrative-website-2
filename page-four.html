YOUR VOYAGE BEGINS

Day 1

WE LEAVE EARTH

Months of training and reaserching the day is finally here. Starship awaits with the new high-tech Dragon capsule specifically designed for your journey to the red planet. After boarding Starship, you will be throttled into the earths exosphere at a speed of 7.5 Kilometres per hour! Shortly after being detached the 7 month breath-taking voyage through deep space begins.

Day 91

THE JOURNEY THOUGH SPACE

Deep in the abyss of space your Dragon capsule floats at a speed of 17500mph- Space X, whilst encountering a colossal group of asteroids, Higley exposing sun rays and deep hues of cosmic radiotin exposure caused by the Sun. During the cruise you may experience lack of communication to your loved ones, and lack of basic necessity’s including water. These factors will contribute to your emotions causing phycological issues..

Day 212

LANDING ON MARS

Touchdown! The long 7-month journey you have embarked on would of seemed like a click of a finger, it will take a while to settle in whilst you enjoy the breath taking views of Mars’ rocky surface. Despite the awkward feeling of the warmer surface and colder air, you will begin research; collecting samples, testing the air and setting up the machines for self-sustain water, food and oxygen. And of course, plant the first flag on mars!

Day 424

LET'S GO BACK HOME

The time is here to head back home. One week flew past and now the Long, difficult and time consuming 7 month journey back begins. After exiting Mars’ Atmosphere the aircraft back would glide using solar power from the sun, combined with combustion power to reach back home.

WELCOME HOME

After a long journey of nearly 15 months, you have finally reached home – earth. After a whole week of research, including climate testing, habituality testing and collecting samples of rock and minerals, researchers can now further progress in studies on how the civilisation can populate the red planet.
